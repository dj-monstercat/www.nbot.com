Brand Ambassador

Who are Brand Ambassadors?

You might have noticed the people with green microphone icons. They are our dedicated, kind, and helpful volunteer Brand Ambassadors (BA) that make plug.dj a great place to hang out and listen to music.

  • Support new community members and answer questions from the community.
  • Moderate communities, especially when staff are not present.
  • Promote plug.dj around the world and introduce new people to the site.

BA Special Projects

Beyond helping within communities, many BAs choose to volunteer and work with a variety of projects to promote and support plug.dj. Here are a few projects they are currently working on:

Support Desk & Live Support

BAs spend time answering support tickets & run a live support community. Read details about it here.

Small Community Support

This group makes themselves available to smaller communities and help promote them on our blog.

Reddit

BAs have helped redesign our subreddit and create a place off-site for continued discussions and support.

SoundCloud

A SoundCloud to promote music BAs have heard on plug.dj. Updated every Thursday.

BA Hangouts

A monthly event to answer questions and meet BAs you see around the site.

Facebook & Twitter

These BAs run the @baplugdj Twitter account and help answer your Facebook wall post questions.

Pinterest

BAs contribute to music & gaming boards to expand our outreach.

Gaming

Getting interviews with streamers, helping out gaming communities, and finding new ways to outreach.

What do BAs earn?

A green name and microphone icon in the chat
Special permissions on the site that help them moderate
The chance to learn valuable new skills and directly apply them to real-world situations
Membership in a fun, special community of fellow BAs from around the world
Direct access to plug.dj administrators
Special avatars just for Brand Ambassadors
